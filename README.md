# CFPB Consumer Complaints Dashboard (2022–2024)

A Power BI dashboard analyzing **over 4M consumer complaints** submitted to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) between **Jan 2022 and Dec 2024**.  
The report highlights complaint trends, response performance, top products, key issues, and company-level patterns.

## Overview
This project analyzes CFPB complaint data to uncover:
- Monthly and yearly complaint trends  
- Timely vs delayed company responses  
- Top products and issues generating the highest complaints  
- Complaint distribution across states  
- Companies with the most complaints and highest delayed responses

- Data Source: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/


The dashboard is fully built in **Power BI** with custom DAX measures.

## Key Highlights
- **Total Complaints:** 4.83M  
- **Timely Responses:** 4.81M  
- **Delayed Responses:** 18.77K  
- **Timely Response Rate:** 99.61%  
- **Companies with only delayed responses:** 762  
- **Top complaint driver:** Credit Reporting (2.77M+ complaints)  

Source: CFPB Public Consumer Complaints Dataset.

---

## Dashboard Insights

### Complaint Trends (2022–2024)
Complaint volumes show a **steady rise** since 2022, indicating growing reporting behavior or increased consumer challenges.

Timely responses closely follow total complaints, holding above **99%** throughout the period.

---

### Timely vs Delayed Responses
- Companies overwhelmingly respond on time (4.81M timely vs 18.77K delayed).  
- However, **762 companies recorded 0% timely responses**, indicating compliance or operational breakdowns.

---

### Products with Most Complaints
Top 3 categories:
1. **Credit Reporting** – 2.77M  
2. **Credit Reporting (Credit Repair)** – 1.25M  
3. **Debt Collection** – 283K  

These categories dominate the dataset and indicate areas needing industry-wide improvement.

---

### Most Reported Issues
- **Incorrect information** on credit reports (1.86M)  
- **Improper use of reports** (1.25M)  
- Issues with companies’ investigation processes  

These highlight weaknesses in data accuracy and dispute resolution.

---

### States with Most Complaints
Top states:
- **Florida (0.66M)**  
- **Texas (0.60M)**  
- **California (0.48M)**  
- **Georgia (0.37M)**  

Large states consistently show higher complaint activity.

---

### Company Insights
**Top companies by complaint volume:**  
- TransUnion, Equifax, Experian (each 1M+ complaints)  
- Followed by major banks (Capital One, JPMorgan Chase, Wells Fargo)

**Highest delayed responses:**  
- EdFinancial Services  
- Bank of America  
- CCS Financial Services  
- Incomm Holdings  
- LexisNexis  

---

## Recommendations
- Strengthen compliance monitoring for companies with consistent delayed responses.  
- Improve data accuracy and dispute handling within credit reporting companies.  
- Target high-complaint product categories (credit reporting & debt collection) with more proactive support.  
- Use state-level trends to identify regions needing targeted consumer protection efforts.  
- Benchmark best-performing companies and share practices with low performers.
